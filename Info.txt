Title: Zig Zag
Gameplay: A ball goes either right or left. Direction change with left mouse click. Game over when player falls off map.
Made as a school assignment
Created by Albin Fyhr

Features:
- Main Menu
- UI
- Simple but extremely hard gameplay
- Slow and speedpads
- Score based on how many coins collected
- Tracks highscore (does not save on exit)

Known bugs:
- Map sometimes creates a small gap between tiles
  Most likely math problem in mapgeneration script
